We collect the following information about you:

Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, username, profile picture, and other information you provide to us when you create an account or use our App.
Content: This includes the photos, captions, comments, and other content you post on our App.
Messaging: This includes your messages and chats with other users.
Information we collect automatically:

Device Information: This includes your device type, operating system version, and unique device identifier.
Usage Information: This includes information about how you use our App, such as the time and date of your visit, the pages you viewed, and the features you used.
Log Data: This includes information that your browser or mobile device automatically transmits to us, such as your IP address, browser type, and referring website.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law.

You have the following choices regarding your information:

You can access, correct, and delete your information by logging into your account and visiting your settings page.

Your information may be transferred to and stored on computers located outside of your country or region. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your information is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
